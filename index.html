WHO WE ARE?

Thokoza Swimming Club is a premier destination for aquatic excellence and community engagement. Established on October 15, 2013, by the Thokoza community, our club is dedicated to promoting water safety, fitness, and personal development. With a commitment to inclusivity, we offer a diverse range of swimming programs tailored to meet the needs of swimmers of all ages and skill levels. It is a registered non-profit organization, which is inspired by a commitment to inclusivity and community empowerment,
